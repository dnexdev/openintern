# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in OpenIntern a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- No spam, malicious PRs, or intentional abuse of the public API

## Enforcement

Report issues to the maintainers via GitHub. Maintainers may warn, block, or ban contributors who violate these standards.
